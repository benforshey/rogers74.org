Main Event

“My goodness—it was AMAZING! Such a huge variety of people came; the decorations were incredible, the venue couldn't have been more gorgeous, the music was fantastic, the food WONDERFUL. One of my highlights was Lonnie Gratz playing the harmonica. I was blown away! The cakewalk was a really fun and unusual twist, except that I lost, just like I used to do at Fruitland Elementary. :) The photo booth was really fun and the elementary alumni pictures were a great addition.”

Margaret Grieve-Fent
